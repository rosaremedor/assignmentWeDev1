Venezuelan Restaurants in Dublin

Not in the cooking mood? Order from these restaurants

Arepas Grill

They consider themselves as the restaurant that serves the most delicious food from Venezuela. Their corn flour dough’s are cooked and turned into Cornbread (Arepa) filled with premium toppings of all kinds. For all the Health buffs, Arepas features Gluten Free, Dairy Free, vegan, and vegetarian choices. Menu: arepas, cachapas, Tequeños, sopus, tres leches cake. Location: 39 Richmond St S, Saint Kevin's, Dublin 2, D02 DC84 Opening Hours: 12:00am - 21:30pm, Tuesday to Sunday Click here to go to their Website

Sweet Churro

Sweet Churro has grown from the love that two Venezuelan business women have for Churros. Using their shrewd business sense, they noticed the lack of Churros in the Irish market. They decided to avail of this opportunity and make their homemade Churros available in Ireland. Their unique love and passion allows them to transform Sweet Churro’s top-secret traditional recipe into crunchy clouds of deliciousness. Menu: Churros, Cachapas, Tequeños, Empanadas Location: Sweet Churro - 3/4 Crow Street. Temple Bar, Dublin. Opening Hours : Monday - Sunday 11:00 am — 7:00pm Click here to go to their Website

Pacheco's Street Food

First Traditional Venezuelan Street Food stall in Irish Village Markets. They sell Arepas, Tequenos, Empanadas, Currently, they are Not operating at Irish Village Markets due to COVID-19 but they are delivering delicious frozen Venezuelan Food and goods to eat when you need to your door!
